Burnley Football Club

Founded: 1882 (originally as Burnley Rovers, renamed Burnley FC the same year)

Location: Burnley, Lancashire

Stadium: Turf Moor (Opened 1883, Capacity ~21,400)

Nicknames: The Clarets (from their claret-and-blue kit)

Historic Note: Burnley were among the 12 founding members of the Football League in 1888. They are one of only a few clubs to have won all four professional divisions in English football.
